Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

Nestd collects your email address and name when you create an account. Your storage box data (box names, locations, and items) is stored securely in Firebase and synced across your devices.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the Nestd service — storing and syncing your inventory data across devices. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

AI Features

The AI image scanning feature is powered by Anthropic's Claude API using your own API key. Images you scan are sent directly to Anthropic and are not stored by Nestd.

Data Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@nestd.net.
